About Us

We are MediHive—your trusted partners in health. As a non-profit organization, we’re dedicated to supporting you with reliable healthcare information and guidance whenever you need it. Our team includes four core leaders, along with volunteer specialists and doctors who generously share their expertise. Together, we provide up-to-date health insights and direct access to healthcare professionals for personalized support. We're committed to continuously improving our services to meet your health needs.
Got questions or concerns? Contact us by clicking the button below.

MediHive: Your Health, Our Mission.

Our Mission

To deliver accurate, up-to-date information on symptoms, illnesses, and diseases, while providing practical health guides, tips, and accessible digital treatments from qualified medical professionals. Our goal is to support visitors in understanding and managing their health with reliable resources and expert guidance tailored to their needs.

Our Vision

To create a healthier world by empowering individuals, including those in Myanmar, with accessible medical knowledge and support. We envision a future where everyone has the resources they need for better health, achieved through spreading healthcare knowledge, hosting meet-and-greet events, and establishing in-person stations to provide compassionate, hands-on assistance to those in need.
